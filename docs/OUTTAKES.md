## Wall of Infamy (Lovingly Bad Ideas)

**Competition G (Milestone PDF) — "The Coin"**
The Goldsmith proposed a two-sided coin layout with the inscription "E PLURIBUS FEATURES" and "IN PRACTICE WE TRUST." We love you, Goldsmith. We're still not minting currency.

**Competition G — "The Colophon"**
The Engraver proposed a book colophon style: "First Edition — One of One." Sir, this is a PDF, not a Gutenberg Bible. (Although "One of One" is actually kind of beautiful and we should revisit this.)

**Session 5 — The Great Uncommitted Work Crisis**
Three competitions ran overnight. Beautiful work was produced. *None of it was committed.* Session ended. Context was lost. This event is why VISION.md exists. The river remembers its floods.

**Session 2 — The Liquid Glass Challenge**
Max: "This still doesn't look like Apple Health."
Claude: *rebuilds the entire visual system in one session*
The audacity of that feedback. The audacity of that response. Both legendary.

**Competition H (Internal Audit) — Token Budget Meeting**
The Efficiency Engineer suggested "No sub-agents for tasks under 30 seconds." This was written in a context that costs tokens. The irony was not lost on anyone.
